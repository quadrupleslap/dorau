The necrotic dragon finally lays still. For a short time, everything is silent.

You hear movement behind you, and you turn around. Shayla is rousing! She looks
at you, through half-lidded eyes, and then smiles. But there is something...
different.... in that smile... and was her eyes always red...?

Her left arm suddenly snakes out, and a torrent of white fire projects from it,
straight at you!
